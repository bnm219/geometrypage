Pineaple Flowers

Mini Fruit Flowers

When growing its fruit the pineapple plant produces over 200 flowers varying in color from lavender, through to light purple and red. The individual scale like fruits of these flowers then join together to create the pineapple. An individual pineapple can take over two years to grow, although they are usually picked slightly earlier than this.
