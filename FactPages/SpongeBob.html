Who Lives In A Pinapple Under The Sea?

SpongeBob Squarepants

124 Conch Street[1] is the address of the pineapple house where SpongeBob SquarePants and his pet snail Gary and pet scallop Shelley live in. The house is three stories high and fully furnished. It first appears in the episode "Help Wanted." The pineapple itself originates from land, when it fell off of a ship navigating above the sea, as seen in the TV special "Truth or Square."
